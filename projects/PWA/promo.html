Educational PWA

Explore the solar system in small, memorable lessons.

Pocket Planetarium turns a phone, tablet, or laptop into a compact astronomy guide with offline lessons, audio playback, a quiz, and progress tracking.

Install

Download instructions

  1. Open the app from a secure web address or local server.
  2. Use the Install button when it appears, or choose “Add to Home Screen” from the browser menu.
  3. Reopen Pocket Planetarium from the device launcher and keep learning offline.
